Tomonaga Ueno, Assistant Professor in the Graduate School of Engineering at Nagoya University, developed a black functional material said to be between 0.5 and 10 times lighter than air that floats simply by applying heat. The article covers his work developing carbon nanotube materials and building a company to bring them to market.
