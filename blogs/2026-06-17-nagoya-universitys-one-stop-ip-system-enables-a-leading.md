An interview with Professor Eiji Shamoto and Chief Research Administrator Noriaki Kojima on how Nagoya University's "one-stop IP system" helps researchers commercialize innovations, providing continuous support from patent filing through company introductions and contract negotiation with a single administrator handling the process. Shamoto stresses that intellectual property forms the foundation of trust between academia and industry.
